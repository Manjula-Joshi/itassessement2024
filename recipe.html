Matcha Latte Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1 cup milk (any type - dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 teaspoon honey or sweetener of choice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the matcha powder and hot water until the matcha is fully dissolved and there are no clumps.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until steaming but not boiling.
  3. Pour the steamed milk into a mug.
  4. Add the dissolved matcha mixture to the mug with the steamed milk.
  5. If desired, add honey or sweetener of choice to the matcha latte and stir until dissolved.
  6. Using a frother or whisk, froth the matcha latte until foamy.
  7. Enjoy your delicious matcha latte!

Burrito Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Cooked rice
  • Black beans
  • Grilled chicken or beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Lettuce, chopped
  • Tomatoes, diced
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Warm the tortillas in a skillet or microwave.
  2. Layer cooked rice, black beans, grilled chicken or beef, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, avocado, and cilantro in the center of each tortilla.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Roll up the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Serve with lime wedges on the side.

Classic Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until brown. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, salt, pepper, and parsley. Simmer for 30 to 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.

Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella and tomato slices on top of the sauce.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  9. Slice and serve hot.

Sourdough Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup active sourdough starter
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Extra flour for dusting

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sourdough starter, bread flour, and warm water. Stir until a shaggy dough forms.
  2. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Sprinkle the salt over the dough and knead it for about 10 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise at room temperature for about 6-8 hours, or until doubled in size.
  5. Once the dough has doubled in size, punch it down and shape it into a round loaf.
  6. Place the loaf on a lightly floured surface, cover it with a kitchen towel, and let it rise for another 2-4 hours, or until puffy.
  7. Preheat your oven to 450째F (230째C). Place a Dutch oven or baking dish in the oven to preheat.
  8. Carefully transfer the risen loaf to the preheated Dutch oven or baking dish. Score the top of the loaf with a sharp knife.
  9. Cover the Dutch oven with its lid (or use aluminum foil to cover the baking dish) and bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove the lid (or foil) and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the loaf is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
  11. Let the bread cool on a wire rack before slicing and serving.

Tiramisu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24-30 ladyfinger cookies (savoiardi)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Dark chocolate shavings, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a heatproof bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water, making sure the bottom of the bowl does not touch the water.
  2. Whisk the egg yolk mixture continuously until it becomes thick and pale yellow, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Remove the bowl from the heat and whisk in the mascarpone cheese until smooth. Let the mixture cool slightly.
  4. In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined.
  6. In a shallow dish, combine the brewed coffee and coffee liqueur (if using).
  7. Dip each ladyfinger cookie into the coffee mixture for a few seconds, making sure not to soak them completely.
  8. Arrange a layer of dipped ladyfinger cookies in the bottom of a serving dish or 9x13-inch baking dish.
  9. Spoon half of the mascarpone mixture over the layer of ladyfingers and spread it evenly.
  10. Repeat with another layer of dipped ladyfinger cookies and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  11. Cover the tiramisu and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to set.
  12. Before serving, dust the top of the tiramisu with unsweetened cocoa powder and garnish with dark chocolate shavings, if desired.
  13. Slice and serve chilled.

Yakitori Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sake (Japanese rice wine)
  • 2 tablespoons mirin (sweet Japanese rice wine)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Vegetables for skewering (optional): bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, zucchini, etc.
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together the soy sauce, sake, mirin, sugar, garlic, and ginger to make the yakitori sauce.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the sauce and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 4 hours in the refrigerator.
  3. If using vegetables, thread them onto skewers alternating with the marinated chicken pieces.
  4. Preheat your grill or broiler to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill or broil the skewers, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred on the edges, about 8-10 minutes.
  6. Brush the skewers with any remaining marinade during cooking.
  7. Remove the skewers from the grill or broiler and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Serve the yakitori skewers hot, optionally garnished with chopped green onions or sesame seeds.

Onigiri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Filling of your choice (e.g., pickled plum, grilled salmon, tuna salad)
  • Salt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Drain well.
  2. In a rice cooker, combine the rinsed rice and water. Cook according to the rice cooker's instructions.
  3. While the rice is still hot, mix in a pinch of salt (if desired) to season it.
  4. Wet your hands to prevent sticking, then scoop a small amount of rice into your palm.
  5. Press a small indentation in the center of the rice and place a small amount of filling into the indentation.
  6. Mold the rice around the filling, shaping it into a triangle or ball.
  7. Wrap a strip of nori seaweed around the onigiri to hold it together.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining rice and filling.
  9. Onigiri can be served immediately or stored wrapped in plastic wrap for later enjoyment.

Enjoy your delicious homemade onigiri!
